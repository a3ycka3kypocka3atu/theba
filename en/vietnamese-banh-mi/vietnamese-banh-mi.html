Bánh Mì is one of Vietnam's most famous specialities, bringing together Vietnamese flavour and French baking tradition in one iconic sandwich. It developed during the French period in Vietnam, when the baguette was gradually adapted to local tastes and ingredients.

The result became a symbol of modern Vietnamese food culture and one of the world's best-loved street food dishes.

At the centre of Bánh Mì is a light, crisp baguette with a soft, airy inside. It is filled with quality protein, fresh vegetables, herbs and savoury accents that create a balanced mix of flavour and texture.

What makes Bánh Mì so memorable is the combination of freshness, juiciness and crunch in every bite. In Vietnam, it can be found on street corners throughout the day, from quick breakfasts to light lunches and evening snacks.

Each region has its own versions and recipes, shaped by local food traditions and available ingredients.

Our Vietnamese Bánh Mì is available with beef, grilled pork belly or eggs. Each option brings its own character while preserving the authentic spirit of this legendary Vietnamese dish.

Fresh preparation, quality ingredients and traditional inspiration come together in a sandwich that brings the real taste of Vietnam closer to the table.
