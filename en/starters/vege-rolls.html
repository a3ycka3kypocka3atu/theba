Vege Rolls

A light vegetarian starter with crisp rolls, fresh vegetables and delicate glass noodles.

Price89 CZK
Portion5 pcs
StyleVegetarian starter

Vege Rolls are a light and fresh vegetarian speciality inspired by traditional Vietnamese cooking. These crisp rolls are prepared with vegetables and glass noodles, creating a balanced combination of flavour and texture.

The simple composition lets the natural taste of each ingredient come through, so the dish feels light while still being pleasantly satisfying.

Vegetable rolls have a long tradition in Vietnam and are often served as a starter or as a small snack during the day. They are loved for freshness, simplicity and the ability to use seasonal ingredients.

Every region brings its own variations, shaped by local food habits and available produce.

Vege Rolls are an ideal choice for vegetarian guests and for anyone who wants to enjoy a light dish inspired by the authentic flavours of Vietnam.

Inside the plate

  • Cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Glass noodles
  • Crisp wrapper
  • Vegetarian filling
