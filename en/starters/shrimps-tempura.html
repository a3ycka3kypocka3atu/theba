Shrimps Tempura

Tender shrimps wrapped in a light crisp coating, inspired by classic Asian tempura.

Price129 CZK
Portion2 pcs
StyleShrimps tempura

Shrimps Tempura is a popular speciality inspired by Asian gastronomy, bringing together tender shrimps and a light, crisp batter.

Thanks to this style of preparation, the shrimps keep their juiciness and natural flavour while the outside develops a characteristic golden texture.

Tempura has its roots in Japanese cuisine, where it gradually became a symbol of light frying with a strong focus on ingredient quality. Today it is one of the most popular Asian starters and appears in restaurants around the world.

Shrimps Tempura offers a polished balance of tenderness, crunch and elegance.

It works beautifully as a light starter or as part of shared dining, where guests can explore a wider range of Asian flavours.
