Fresh Spring Rolls

Fresh rice paper rolls with vegetables, herbs, rice noodles and sesame.

Price129 CZK
StyleFresh spring rolls
ChoiceShrimps, beef, pork belly or eggs

Fresh Spring Rolls are among the most traditional and health-conscious specialities of Vietnamese cuisine.

Unlike fried rolls, they are prepared with fresh rice paper that wraps vegetables, herbs, rice noodles and other quality ingredients.

Because the dish is prepared fresh, it keeps the lightness, brightness and natural flavour of the ingredients. Salad, herbs, carrot, kohlrabi and rice noodles create a balanced whole that is loved in Vietnam and by fans of fresh eating around the world.

Fresh Spring Rolls reflect the philosophy of Vietnamese cuisine: freshness, balance and quality ingredients.

The option to choose different fillings brings variety and lets each guest discover their own favourite combination.

Inside the roll

  • Rice paper
  • Salad and herbs
  • Carrot and kohlrabi
  • Rice noodles
  • Sesame
