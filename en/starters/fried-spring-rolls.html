Fried Spring Rolls

Crispy fried rolls with a rich filling inspired by traditional Vietnamese home cooking.

Price109 CZK
Portion2 pcs
StyleFried spring rolls

Fried Spring Rolls are among the best-known Vietnamese starters and are part of family celebrations, holidays and everyday dining.

Traditional fried rolls are valued for their golden, crisp shell and generous filling, where several ingredients come together in a balanced whole.

Inside the rolls are meat, egg, vegetables, glass noodles and other traditional ingredients. During frying, the rolls develop their characteristic texture and aroma, which has helped make them one of the most popular Vietnamese dishes around the world.

In Vietnam, recipes are often passed from generation to generation, and each family has its own way of preparing them.

Fried Spring Rolls are an authentic expression of Vietnamese cooking, combining simple ingredients with long tradition and careful preparation.

Inside the roll

  • Pork
  • Egg
  • Carrot
  • Kohlrabi
  • Glass noodles
