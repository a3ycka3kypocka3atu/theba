Red Curry with Rice

A fragrant red curry with rice, fresh vegetables, basil and a choice of chicken, beef, shrimps or tofu.

Price249 CZK
StyleRed curry with rice
ChoiceChicken, beef, shrimps or tofu

Red Curry is one of Southeast Asia's most recognisable curry dishes, loved for its deep aroma, warm colour and the balance between sweet, spicy and fresh notes.

The character of the dish comes from red curry paste, traditionally built from chilli, herbs and spices. It gives the sauce both its colour and its rounded fragrance.

Our Red Curry with Rice pairs a gently spicy curry sauce with rice, vegetables and basil. Zucchini, bell pepper, carrot, onion, mushrooms and bean pods bring colour, texture and freshness to the bowl.

You can choose chicken, beef, shrimps or tofu, so the dish can be rich, light, seafood-led or vegetarian. It is a warm, aromatic curry made for guests who enjoy flavour with a little heat.
