Yellow Curry with Rice

A softer golden curry with rice, fresh vegetables and a warm aromatic profile without strong heat.

Price249 CZK
StyleYellow curry with rice
ChoiceChicken, beef, shrimps or tofu

Yellow Curry is a gentler expression of Asian curry cooking. Its golden colour comes from turmeric and other aromatic ingredients often used across Southeast Asian recipes.

This dish combines fragrant curry sauce with rice, fresh vegetables and carefully chosen ingredients. Zucchini, bell pepper, carrot, onion, mushrooms and bean pods create a balanced mix of textures, while basil adds freshness.

The result is mild, aromatic and full of warm exotic notes. Yellow Curry is a natural choice for guests who want curry richness without a strong spicy edge.

Choose chicken, beef, shrimps or tofu to make the dish your own and explore a softer side of Asian curry.
