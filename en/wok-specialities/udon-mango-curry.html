Udon Mango Curry

Udon noodles in a creamy mango curry sauce with vegetables, mushrooms, bell pepper and sesame.

Price249 CZK
StyleMango curry udon
ChoiceChicken, beef, shrimps or tofu

Udon Mango Curry brings together soft Asian noodles and a creamy mango curry sauce. The dish combines tropical sweetness with aromatic spice for a flavour that feels both comforting and original.

The base is udon noodles with fresh vegetables, mushrooms, bell pepper and sesame. Mango curry sauce gives the bowl its smooth texture, gentle sweetness and warm spiced character.

It is a modern Asian-style dish for guests who enjoy something a little different: familiar noodles, but with a brighter and more exotic curry profile.

Choose chicken, beef, shrimps or tofu. Each option changes the character of the bowl while keeping the mango curry at the centre.

  • Vegetables
