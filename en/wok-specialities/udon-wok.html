Udon Wok

Stir-fried udon noodles with fresh vegetables, sesame and your choice of chicken, beef, shrimps or tofu.

Price229 CZK
StyleStir-fried udon noodles
ChoiceChicken, beef, shrimps or tofu

Udon Wok is inspired by Asian cooking built around fresh ingredients, fast preparation and balanced flavour. Udon noodles come from Japan and are loved for their soft texture and their ability to absorb sauces and aromas.

Here, udon noodles are stir-fried with zucchini, carrot, onion, bean pods, bell pepper and mushrooms. Wok cooking keeps the vegetables lively while bringing everything together in one warm, satisfying dish.

Sesame adds a gentle nutty aroma and extra depth. Choose chicken, beef, shrimps or tofu to shape the dish around your own taste.

Udon Wok is simple, fresh and flexible: a modern Asian noodle dish with comfort, texture and clean flavour.

Inside the bowl

  • Carrot
  • Mushrooms
  • Sesame
