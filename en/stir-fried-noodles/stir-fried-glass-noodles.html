Stir-Fried Glass Noodles

Glass noodles stir-fried with egg, vegetables, bean sprouts and fried onion.

Price239 CZK
StyleWok-fried glass noodles
ChoiceChicken, beef or shrimps

Stir-Fried Glass Noodles are inspired by Asian cooking, where glass noodles are valued for their delicate texture and ability to absorb flavour.

The dish combines glass noodles with egg, carrot, onion, leek, bean pods, bean sprouts and fried onion. Each ingredient adds its own texture, from soft noodles to crisp vegetables.

Wok preparation keeps the vegetables lively while bringing all parts of the dish together into one balanced plate.

Choose chicken, beef or shrimps depending on the flavour you want. Stir-Fried Glass Noodles feel light, but still generous and satisfying.

Inside the wok

  • Glass noodles
  • Egg
  • Carrot and onion
  • Leek and bean pods
  • Fried onion
