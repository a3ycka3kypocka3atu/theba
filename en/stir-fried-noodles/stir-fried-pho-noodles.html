Stir-Fried Pho Noodles

Wide pho noodles stir-fried with egg, vegetables, bean sprouts and fried onion.

Price239 CZK
StyleWok-fried pho noodles
ChoiceChicken, beef or shrimps

Stir-Fried Pho Noodles are a wok-prepared version of Vietnam's familiar rice noodles, giving the dish a deeper flavour and lightly toasted aroma.

The base is wide pho noodles, known for their soft texture and ability to take on the flavour of surrounding ingredients.

The noodles are stir-fried with egg, carrot, onion, leek, bean pods, bean sprouts and fried onion. The result is fresh, satisfying and full of texture without feeling heavy.

Choose chicken, beef or shrimps to shape the dish to your taste. It is a modern, quick and aromatic way to enjoy the character of Vietnamese noodles.

Inside the wok

  • Pho noodles
  • Egg
  • Carrot and onion
  • Leek and bean pods
  • Bean sprouts
