Bun Bo Nam Bo

A southern Vietnamese noodle bowl where beef, herbs, peanuts, salad and fried onion come together in a fresh, satisfying balance.

Price229 CZK
StyleBeef noodle bowl
AddressOdborů 3, Prague

Bun Bo Nam Bo is one of the most loved dishes from southern Vietnam. It is not a soup, and it is not just a salad. It sits beautifully in between: rice noodles, juicy beef, fresh greens, herbs, bean sprouts, peanuts and crisp fried onion.

Each layer brings something different. The beef gives warmth and richness, the herbs bring lift, the peanuts add crunch and depth, and the noodles make the bowl soft and satisfying.

That balance is a big part of Vietnamese cooking. A good bún bowl should feel generous but not heavy, colourful but not fussy, and fresh from the first bite to the last.

For guests in Prague looking for a complete Vietnamese meal with texture, freshness and comfort, Bun Bo Nam Bo is one of the easiest dishes to love.
