Bun Nem

Crispy fried Nem rolls served with rice noodles, fresh salad, herbs and sweet-sour sauce: crunchy, bright and deeply satisfying.

Price229 CZK
StyleNem rolls and noodles
AddressOdborů 3, Prague

Bun Nem is a Vietnamese favourite because it brings two pleasures together in one bowl: the crunch of fried Nem rolls and the lightness of rice noodles, herbs and fresh vegetables.

The fried rolls add richness and texture, while the noodles keep the dish soft and easy to eat. Salad, herbs, mung bean sprouts and sweet-sour sauce bring freshness and balance, so the bowl never feels heavy.

In Vietnam, Nem rolls often appear at family gatherings, celebrations and shared meals. They carry a sense of tradition, but they also work beautifully as everyday comfort food.

Bun Nem is ideal for guests who want something crisp, fresh and full of contrast. Every bite moves between crunch, softness, herbs and sauce, which is exactly why this dish has become loved far beyond Vietnam.
