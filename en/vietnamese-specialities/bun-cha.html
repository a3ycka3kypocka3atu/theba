Bun Cha

A Hanoi classic with grilled pork, rice noodles, sesame and fresh herbs: smoky, fragrant and unmistakably Vietnamese.

Price249 CZK
StyleGrilled pork
AddressOdborů 3, Prague

Bun Cha is one of Hanoi's most iconic dishes. It is built around grilled pork, rice noodles, fresh herbs and vegetables, with the aroma of the grill giving the dish its unmistakable character.

The beauty of Bun Cha is in the contrast: warm grilled meat with soft noodles, fresh herbs with a savoury depth, sesame and fried onion for extra texture.

In Vietnam, Bun Cha is closely tied to street food culture. It is the kind of dish people gather around at lunch, often served simply but remembered for its balance and fragrance.

At The Ba, Bun Cha is a direct way to taste the spirit of Vietnamese food: fresh ingredients, satisfying grilled flavour and a clean finish that keeps the bowl lively.
