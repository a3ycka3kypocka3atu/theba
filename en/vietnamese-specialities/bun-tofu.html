Bun Tofu

A fresh vegetarian bowl with rice noodles, tofu, herbs, vegetables, bean sprouts and peanuts, made for balance rather than compromise.

Price219 CZK
StyleVegetarian bowl
AddressOdborů 3, Prague

Bun Tofu shows how satisfying Vietnamese vegetarian food can be. It is light, colourful and full of texture, with rice noodles and tofu forming a gentle base for herbs, vegetables, bean sprouts and peanuts.

Tofu brings softness and substance, while the fresh ingredients make the bowl bright and aromatic. Peanuts add crunch and richness, giving the dish a complete, rounded feel without relying on meat.

Plant-based cooking has a long place in Vietnamese food culture, shaped in part by Buddhist traditions and by the everyday use of fresh vegetables, herbs and simple natural ingredients.

Bun Tofu is a strong choice for vegetarian guests, but it is also for anyone who wants a clean, refreshing Vietnamese meal that still feels generous and full of flavour.
