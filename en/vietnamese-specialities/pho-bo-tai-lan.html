Pho Bo Tai Lan

A bolder take on beef pho, with aromatic broth and stir-fried beef that brings extra depth, warmth and fragrance to the bowl.

Price259 CZK
StyleStir-fried beef pho
AddressOdborů 3, Prague

Pho Bo Tai Lan starts with the same Vietnamese respect for broth as classic pho, then adds a richer layer of flavour through stir-fried beef. The meat is quickly cooked to bring out a deeper aroma before it meets the hot broth and soft rice noodles.

The result is fuller and more expressive than a gentle classic pho. Slow-cooked beef broth gives the dish its body, while onion, coriander and mung bean sprouts keep the bowl fresh, bright and balanced.

Pho has many faces across Vietnam. Some bowls are delicate, some are more fragrant, and some carry a stronger savoury note from the way the meat is prepared. Pho Bo Tai Lan belongs to that more intense side of the tradition.

It is a good choice for guests who already love pho and want something with a little more character: familiar, deeply Vietnamese, but with the satisfying aroma of beef from the wok.

Inside the bowl

  • Stir-fried beef
  • Beef broth
  • Rice noodles
  • Coriander
  • Bean sprouts
