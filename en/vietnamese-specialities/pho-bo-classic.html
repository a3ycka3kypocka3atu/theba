Pho Bo Classic

A deeply comforting beef pho built around a slow-cooked broth, tender beef, rice noodles and the fresh lift of coriander and bean sprouts.

Price229 CZK
StyleClassic beef pho
AddressOdborů 3, Prague

Pho Bo Classic is one of the best-known dishes in Vietnamese cooking, and for good reason. It takes simple ingredients and gives them time: bones, beef, spices and aromatics are slowly brought together until the broth becomes clear, rounded and full of quiet depth.

The bowl is finished with rice noodles, beef, onion, coriander and crisp mung bean sprouts. Nothing in pho is there by accident. The noodles give softness, the herbs bring brightness, and the sprouts add the fresh crunch that keeps each spoonful balanced.

In Vietnam, pho is more than a soup. It is breakfast, lunch, comfort food and a daily ritual shared by families, workers and travellers. Every cook has a slightly different touch, but the heart of the dish stays the same: patience, fragrance and balance.

Our Pho Bo Classic is made for guests who want the clean, traditional expression of Vietnamese pho in Prague: warm, aromatic, generous and easy to come back to.
