Pho Ga

A lighter chicken pho with a clean, delicate broth, rice noodles, tender chicken and the fresh aroma of coriander and bean sprouts.

Price219 CZK
StyleChicken pho
AddressOdborů 3, Prague

Pho Ga is the gentle side of Vietnamese pho. It is built around a chicken broth that is cooked slowly enough to become fragrant and rounded, but still clear and light on the palate.

Rice noodles, chicken, onion, coriander and mung bean sprouts turn the broth into a complete bowl: comforting without being heavy, aromatic without being overwhelming, and fresh enough to work at almost any time of day.

In Vietnam, chicken pho is part of everyday food culture. It can be breakfast before work, a simple lunch, or an easy dinner when you want something warm and nourishing.

Our Pho Ga is made for guests who enjoy balance: tender chicken, soft noodles, bright herbs and a broth that feels calm, honest and deeply Vietnamese.
