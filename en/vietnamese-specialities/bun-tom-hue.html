Bun Tom Hue

A fresh rice noodle bowl inspired by the cooking of central Vietnam, with shrimps, herbs, vegetables and crisp bean sprouts.

Price229 CZK
StyleShrimp and rice noodles
AddressOdborů 3, Prague

Bun Tom Hue draws its inspiration from central Vietnam, a region known for vivid flavours, careful presentation and a strong culinary identity. The historic city of Hue shaped many dishes that still feel special today.

This bowl brings together rice noodles, shrimps, herbs, vegetables and bean sprouts. The shrimps give a gentle sea sweetness, while the herbs and vegetables keep the dish clean, fragrant and refreshing.

Hue-style cooking is often about balance: enough aroma to feel distinctive, enough freshness to stay light, and enough texture to make each bite interesting.

Bun Tom Hue is a good choice when you want Vietnamese food that feels bright and elegant rather than heavy. It is full of flavour, but still fresh enough for lunch or dinner in the city.
